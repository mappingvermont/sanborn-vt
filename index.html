Mapping Historic Montpelier

In addition to the granite sheds, railroads, and clothes pin factories one would expect from Montpelier in 1915, this Sanborn map includes a candy factory (at the present City Center), an ice house (Cummings St at Elm) and a State Library (the current Capitol City Laundromat).
